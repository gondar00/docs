# What is Stretch AI?

**Stretch AI** is a comprehensive marketing automation platform designed to help businesses manage customer engagement, run targeted campaigns, and automate workflows across multiple channels. It integrates with core business systems like Mindbody and leverages popular communication channels such as Email, SMS, and WhatsApp.

## Core Capabilities

Stretch AI is built around several key functional areas:

1.  **Multi-Channel Communication**: Seamlessly engage customers via Email, SMS, and WhatsApp.
2.  **Audience Segmentation**: Create highly specific target groups using Dynamic and Static Lists based on customer data.
3.  **Marketing Automation**: Utilize pre-built and custom automation workflows to nurture leads and manage client lifecycles.
4.  **Performance Monitoring**: Track key metrics like revenue, delivery rates, and engagement through a centralized Dashboard.
5.  **Integration**: Connect with essential business tools like Mindbody, Meta Ads, and Google Ads for synchronized data and enhanced functionality.

## Target Users

The platform is designed for marketing specialists, business owners, and operations managers who need to streamline their customer communication and drive revenue through automated marketing efforts.
