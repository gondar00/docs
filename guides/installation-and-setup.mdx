# Installation and Setup

Stretch AI is a cloud-based platform, meaning there is no complex software installation required. Access is granted directly through your web browser.

## Initial Access

1.  Navigate to the Stretch AI application URL: `https://app.getstretch.ai/`.
2.  Enter your registered email address.

<img src="/images/screenshots/onboarding/login-page.webp" alt="Login Page" />

3.  A one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your email for secure, passwordless login.
4.  Enter the 6-digit OTP to access your dashboard.

<img src="/images/screenshots/onboarding/otp-verification.webp" alt="OTP Verification" />

## Core Onboarding and Configuration

The initial setup of Stretch AI revolves around connecting the platform to your core business systems and communication channels via the **Integrations** section. This process is crucial for enabling data synchronization and multi-channel engagement.

### Core Onboarding Integrations

Prioritize connecting these key services for a successful launch:

| Integration | Purpose | Category |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mindbody** | Sync clients, services, staff, and contracts. This is the foundation for personalized and event-triggered automations. | Ecommerce |
| **WhatsApp Business** | Connect your WhatsApp Business Account to enable sending template messages and managing conversations in the unified inbox. | Messaging |
| **Meta Ads** | Create audiences and manage ad campaigns across Facebook & Instagram. | Advertising |
