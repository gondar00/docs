This section provides a comprehensive overview of the Stretch AI platform, detailing the functionality of each major feature and demonstrating typical user workflows.

## 1. Dashboard and Performance Monitoring

The Dashboard is the starting point, offering a snapshot of business performance and marketing campaign results.

- **Business Summary**: Review Total Revenue and Attributed Revenue.
- **Campaign Metrics**: Analyze Channel Performance (Volume), Engagement (Open/Click Rates), and Revenue Performance across Email, SMS, and WhatsApp.

## 2. Audience Management (Lists)

Effective marketing begins with precise audience segmentation.

- **Dynamic Segmentation**: Create powerful segments based on Mindbody data or other contact properties.
- **Static Imports**: Easily upload external contact lists via CSV.
- **Form-Based Lists**: Automatically capture leads from forms created in the Form Builder.

## 3. Content Creation (Templates)

The platform supports creating reusable content for all communication channels.

- **Email Editor**: Utilize the drag-and-drop editor to design professional email templates.
- **Multi-Channel Support**: Create dedicated templates for SMS and WhatsApp messages.

## 4. Campaign Execution

Launch targeted messages to your segmented audience.

- **One-Time Sends**: Schedule campaigns for a specific date and time.
- **Recurring Campaigns**: Set up automated, repeating messages for ongoing promotions or communications.
- **A/B Testing**: Test different templates or subject lines to optimize performance.

## 5. Automation Workflows

Automate the customer journey from lead capture to retention.

- **Pre-built Library**: Access templates for common scenarios like lead conversion and client engagement.
- **Custom Workflows**: Build custom automations triggered by events (e.g., form submission, class pack expiration).

## 6. Communication (Conversations)

Manage real-time customer interactions in a unified inbox.

- **WhatsApp Inbox**: Centralized management of all WhatsApp conversations.
- **Search and Filter**: Quickly find and manage specific customer chats.

## 7. Data Collection (Form Builder)

Create custom forms for lead generation and data collection.

- **Form Design**: Simple interface for building custom web forms.
- **Data Integration**: Form submissions automatically feed into Lists and can trigger Automations.

## 8. Integrations

Connect Stretch AI to your core business ecosystem.

- **Mindbody**: Sync client and service data.
- **Messaging**: Connect WhatsApp Business.
- **Advertising**: Link Meta Ads and Google Ads for audience synchronization.
