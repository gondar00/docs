# Onboarding and Integrations

The **Integrations** section is the central hub for connecting Stretch AI with your essential business platforms. A successful integration ensures seamless data synchronization for effective campaign and automation execution.

## Key Integrations Overview

Stretch AI categorizes integrations for easy management: **Messaging**, **Advertising**, **eCommerce**, and **Analytics**.

| Integration | Category | Purpose | Connection Process |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mindbody** | eCommerce | Sync clients, services, staff, and contracts. | Enter Mindbody Site ID and retrieve an Activation Code. |
| **WhatsApp Business** | Messaging | Send templates and messages to customers. | Facebook OAuth flow (Connect New Number or Existing App). |
| **Meta Ads** | Advertising | Create audiences and manage ad campaigns. | Facebook OAuth flow (Connect with Facebook). |
| **Google Ads** | Advertising | Sync customer data and optimize ad campaigns. | Direct connection via Google OAuth. |
| **Shopify** | eCommerce | Sync products, customers, and orders. | Direct connection via Shopify OAuth. |

## Step-by-Step Connection Guides

### 1. Connect Mindbody

The Mindbody integration is crucial for syncing core business data.

1.  Navigate to **Integrations** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **Connect** on the Mindbody card.
3.  Enter your **Mindbody Site ID** in the field provided.
4.  Click **Get Activation Code** to proceed with the final authorization step.

> **Tip:** If you are unsure where to find your Site ID, click the "Where to find your Site ID" link for guidance.

### 2. Connect WhatsApp Business

WhatsApp integration enables direct, personalized messaging campaigns.

1.  Navigate to **Integrations** and click **Connect** on the WhatsApp Business card.
2.  A modal will appear with two options: **Connect New Number** or **Connect Existing WhatsApp Business App**.
3.  Selecting either option will initiate the **Facebook OAuth flow**, requiring you to log in to your Facebook account to authorize the connection.

### 3. Connect Meta Ads

Connecting Meta Ads allows you to leverage your customer data for targeted advertising.

1.  Navigate to **Integrations** and click **Connect** on the Meta Ads card.
2.  Click the **Connect with Facebook** button.
3.  You will be redirected to the **Facebook OAuth flow** to log in, select your Ad Account, and grant the necessary permissions.
