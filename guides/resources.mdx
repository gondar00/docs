## WhatsApp Business Platform Pricing

WhatsApp charges on a per-message basis. Pricing varies by country/region and message type. All prices are in USD and effective as of October 1, 2025.

  - [Authentication International Rates](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/pricing/authentication-international-rates)
</Info>

### Message Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Marketing Messages">
    Marketing messages include promotional content, offers, and campaigns. These messages are used to reach customers with updates about your business, new services, or special promotions.

    **Examples:**

    - Monthly newsletters
    - Special offers and discounts
    - New class announcements
    - Event invitations

    Marketing rates apply across Cloud API and Marketing Messages Lite API.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Utility Messages">
    Utility messages are transactional and provide important account updates or information. These are typically triggered by customer actions or account events.

    **Examples:**

    - Booking confirmations
    - Class reminders
    - Payment receipts
    - Account notifications

    Utility messages generally have lower rates than marketing messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authentication Messages">
    Authentication messages are used for account verification, such as one-time passwords (OTP) for login or account security.

    **Examples:**

    - Login verification codes
    - Password reset codes
    - Two-factor authentication
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Service Messages">
    Service messages are customer-initiated conversations where the business responds within 24 hours. These are typically free or have special pricing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Marketing vs Utility: Which Should You Use?

Understanding the difference between Marketing and Utility messages helps you optimize costs and comply with WhatsApp policies.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Use Marketing When" icon="bullhorn">
    - Sending promotional campaigns
    - Announcing new services or classes
    - Sharing special offers
    - Broadcasting newsletters
    - Re-engaging inactive members
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Utility When" icon="bell">
    - Confirming bookings or purchases
    - Sending class reminders
    - Providing account updates
    - Sharing payment receipts
    - Responding to customer inquiries
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **Cost Optimization Tip:** Utility messages are typically 50-80% cheaper than marketing messages. Structure your campaigns to use utility messages for transactional communications and reserve marketing messages for promotional content.
</Tip>

## Cost Optimization Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prioritize Utility Messages">
    Structure your communications to use utility messages whenever possible. Booking confirmations, class reminders, and account notifications qualify as utility messages and cost significantly less than marketing messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Segment Your Audience">
    Send targeted messages to specific segments rather than broadcasting to your entire list. This reduces message volume while improving engagement and ROI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Automation Wisely">
    Set up event-triggered automations for transactional messages (utility) and schedule marketing campaigns strategically to avoid over-messaging.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor Engagement Rates">
    Track which messages get the best response rates and focus your budget on high-performing message types and campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Combine Channels">
    Use WhatsApp for high-priority messages and supplement with lower-cost channels like email for less time-sensitive communications.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Test and Optimize">
    A/B test your message templates and timing to improve engagement rates, ensuring you get maximum value from each message sent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
