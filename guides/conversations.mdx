title: "Conversations"
description: "Manage all your multi-channel customer conversations in a unified inbox."
---

The Conversations feature provides a unified inbox for managing customer interactions, primarily through **WhatsApp**.

## Unified Inbox Features

- **Channel Focus**: Currently focused on managing WhatsApp conversations.
- **Filtering**: Tabs for **All Messages** and **Unread** messages.
- **Search**: Search conversations by user name, email, or mobile number.
- **Empty State**: Displays "No WhatsApp conversations" if the account is new or the WhatsApp Business integration is not active.

> **Note**: To utilize the Conversations feature, the **WhatsApp Business** integration must be successfully connected.

## User Experience

The interface is designed for quick and efficient customer support and engagement, allowing staff to easily track and respond to customer queries in a centralized location.
