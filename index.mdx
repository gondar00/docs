## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Stretch AI?">
    Stretch AI is an AI-powered customer engagement platform designed specifically for fitness and wellness businesses. It helps you automate communications, manage customer relationships, and grow your business through intelligent automation across multiple channels including WhatsApp, SMS, and email.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get started with Stretch AI?">
    Getting started is simple:

    1. Navigate to [app.getstretch.ai](https://app.getstretch.ai)
    2. Sign in with your email (we use passwordless OTP authentication)
    3. Connect your core integrations (Mindbody, WhatsApp Business, Meta Ads)
    4. Start creating campaigns and automations

    Check out our [Installation and Setup guide](/guides/installation-and-setup) for detailed instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What integrations does Stretch AI support?">
    Stretch AI integrates with:

    - **Business Management**: Mindbody, Shopify, Hirebob
    - **Messaging**: WhatsApp Business, Zendesk
    - **Advertising**: Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads
    - **Payments**: UPayments
    - **Analytics**: Google Analytics

    Visit our [Installation and Setup guide](/guides/installation-and-setup) to learn more about each integration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I send WhatsApp messages to my customers?">
    Yes! Stretch AI fully supports WhatsApp Business messaging. You can:

    - Send template messages for notifications and updates
    - Manage conversations in a unified inbox
    - Create automated WhatsApp campaigns
    - Trigger messages based on customer events

    You'll need to connect your WhatsApp Business Account during onboarding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do automations work?">
    Automations in Stretch AI are event-triggered workflows that engage customers at the right time. For example:

    - Send a welcome message when a new client signs up
    - Remind customers about upcoming classes
    - Re-engage inactive members
    - Follow up after class attendance

    Learn more in our [Automations guide](/guides/automations).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a trial period?">
    Yes! Stretch AI offers a trial period so you can explore the platform and see how it fits your business needs. During the trial, you'll have access to all core features. Contact our support team to learn more about trial options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get support?">
    We're here to help! You can reach our support team at [gandharv@getstretch.ai](mailto:gandharv@getstretch.ai). We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create custom message templates?">
    Absolutely! While we provide pre-built templates for common scenarios, you can create custom templates tailored to your brand voice and specific use cases. Templates support dynamic variables, personalization, and rich media.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
